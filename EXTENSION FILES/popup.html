About DeepFake Detector

DeepFake Detector helps you identify manipulated videos across the web.

How to use

  1. Find a video you want to analyze
  2. Right-click on the video
  3. Select "Check for Deepfake" from the menu
  4. View the analysis results

Understanding Results

Our AI model analyzes videos frame-by-frame to detect signs of manipulation. The confidence score indicates how certain the system is about the detection:

⚠️
Deepfake Detected
High confidence that the video has been manipulated
✓
Authentic Video
No significant signs of manipulation detected

Note: No AI system is perfect. Always use critical thinking when evaluating content.
